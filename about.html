About Me

Who is noah boursier?

My goal in life is to change the world and to have a good time doing it.

I have taken two computer science classes and a general engineering class (Principles of Engineering) at Dublin High School as part of an engineering pathway and am currently taking a course about digital electronics as a senior. I am artistic, analytical, stable, realistic, rational, resourceful and creative. I use the new things I learn in my projects and endeavors all the time.

Though my family is big into windsurfing, at the beach you are much more likely to find me digging swaths of interconnected holes rather than playing out on the water. At home I'll lose track of time while building with legos or working on robots. Though I may not always have tools or materials, I will always find a way to create. I have come to see every limitation of resources is an opportunity for resourcefulness. I have worked with wood, metal, glass, software, etc.

I enjoy designing and building, and have experience with a variety of tools. I've created a crossbow with a trigger system out of a heavy duty cardboard pipe, a bow, a little mechanism that launches quarters, traps, cardboard fortresses, all kinds of art, computers, a tree house, robots, videogames with various engines, etc...

I feel like I could do anything if I put my mind to it, the only limiting factor is time.
