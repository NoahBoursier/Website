About Me

Who is noah boursier?

My goal in life is to change the world and to have a good time doing it.

I have taken two computer science classes and a general engineering class (Principles of Engineering) at Dublin High School as part of an engineering pathway. I am artistic, analytical, stable, realistic, rational, resourceful and creative. I use the new things I learn in my projects and endeavors all the time.

Though my family is big into windsurfing, at the beach you are much more likely to find me digging swaths of interconnected holes rather than playing out on the water. At home I'll lose track of time while building with legos or working on robots. Though I may not always have tools or materials, I will always find a way to create. I have come to see every limitation of resources is an opportunity for resourcefulness. I have worked with wood, metal, glass, software, etc.

I enjoy designing and building, and have experience with a variety of tools. I've created a crossbow with a trigger system out of a heavy duty cardboard pipe, a bow, a little mechanism that launches quarters, traps, cardboard fortresses, all kinds of art, computers, a tree house, videogames with various engines, etc...

I feel like I could do anything if I put my mind to it, the only limiting factor is time.

Random Details

Hobbies

I like hiking, biking, gaming, spending time with friends, building robots, playing with digital electronics, coding, running, making art, and a little bit of photography. I like to chat about philosophy or theoretical physics because everyone has something different to add to my understanding.

Philosophy

Being the cold logical person that I am, and not seeing any evidence for a higher power, I have decided that I don't really think there is one. This means that my life probably has no ultimate purpose. Though some may find this depressing, to me it just means that I get to come up with my own purpose.
